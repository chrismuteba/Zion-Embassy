Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our church and services.

What should I expect on my first visit?

We're excited to welcome you! Our services are warm and welcoming. You can expect contemporary worship music, biblical teaching, and friendly people. Dress is casual, and we have programs for children during the service. The service typically lasts about 90 minutes.

Do you have programs for children?

Yes! We have Children's Church for ages 3-12 during our Sunday morning service. Our trained volunteers provide age-appropriate Bible lessons, games, and activities in a safe, fun environment. Nursery care is also available for infants and toddlers.

How can I get involved in ministry?

We'd love to help you find your place to serve! Visit our Ministries page to learn about our various ministry opportunities, or speak with Pastor Emmanuel or Miss Narshley after a service. We also have a volunteer application process for certain ministries.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have free parking available on site. Our parking area can accommodate most vehicles, and we have volunteers to help direct traffic during busy services.

Do you offer baptism?

Absolutely! We believe in baptism by immersion as a public declaration of faith. If you're interested in being baptized, please speak with Pastor Emmanuel to discuss your decision and schedule your baptism.

How can I request prayer?

You can submit prayer requests through our contact form, speak with any of our pastoral team members, or join us for our Friday evening prayer meetings. We also have a prayer chain for urgent requests.
