Our Story

Discover the journey of faith that has shaped our church community and continues to guide us today.

Founded with a heart for God and a vision for community transformation, The Ekklesia Zion Embassy began as a small gathering of believers united by a shared passion for the Pentecostal faith and the manifestation of God's glory.

Through years of faithful prayer, dynamic worship, and committed outreach, our church has grown into a vibrant spiritual home for families and individuals seeking a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ. We remain rooted in our Pentecostal heritage while embracing the fresh move of the Holy Spirit in our contemporary context.

Our journey has been marked by God's faithfulness, miraculous interventions, and the transformation of countless lives. We continue to be amazed by what God is doing in and through our church family.

Our Mission & Vision

Our purpose and direction are clearly defined by our mission and vision statements.

Our Mission

"To create the kingdom family of the chosen people of God and manifest the glory of the Kingdom through worship, discipleship, and service."

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope in our community, raising disciples who live out the fullness of the Holy Spirit's power, transforming lives through the gospel of Jesus Christ and impacting our generation for God's kingdom.

Our Core Beliefs

As a Pentecostal/Charismatic church, we hold to these foundational truths that guide our faith and practice.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and the final authority for faith and life. It is our guide for all matters of doctrine and Christian living.

Salvation

We believe in salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ, who died for our sins and rose again. Salvation is a free gift available to all who believe.

Holy Spirit

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with the evidence of speaking in tongues and the operation of spiritual gifts for the edification of the church.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, discipleship, and spread the gospel to all nations.

Divine Healing

We believe in divine healing through the redemptive work of Christ on the cross and the power of prayer and faith.

Second Coming

We believe in the imminent return of Jesus Christ and the resurrection of both the saved and the lost for eternal judgment.
