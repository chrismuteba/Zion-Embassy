Our Main Ministries

Each ministry is designed to help you grow spiritually while serving others and building community.

Worship & Arts

Our worship team leads the congregation into God's presence through music, song, and creative arts. We believe worship is a lifestyle, not just a Sunday experience.

What We Do:

  • • Lead Sunday worship services
  • • Organize special worship nights
  • • Train musicians and vocalists
  • • Creative arts and drama

Get Involved:

Whether you play an instrument, sing, or have a heart for creative expression, there's a place for you in our worship ministry.

Youth Ministry

Engaging young people aged 13-25 with relevant biblical teaching, fun activities, and a community where they can grow in their faith and discover their purpose.

What We Do:

  • • Weekly youth gatherings
  • • Summer camps and retreats
  • • Mentorship programs
  • • Community service projects

Meeting Times:

Fridays 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
Special events throughout the month

Outreach Ministry

Sharing God's love through community service, evangelism, and missions work. We believe in being the hands and feet of Jesus in our community.

What We Do:

  • • Food distribution programs
  • • Community clean-up initiatives
  • • Prison ministry
  • • Street evangelism

Impact:

Over 200 families served monthly through our food program and countless lives touched through our outreach efforts.

Children's Church

A fun, safe environment where children ages 3-12 learn about Jesus at their own level through interactive lessons, games, and activities.

What We Offer:

  • • Age-appropriate Bible lessons
  • • Interactive games and activities
  • • Arts and crafts
  • • Character building programs

Safety First:

All volunteers are background checked and trained in child safety protocols.
